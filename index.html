Journal

March 13 was the day when we were introduced to the vibrant team at R Web Solutions. We had the opportunity to greet and connect with all the employees, getting a sense of the welcoming and supportive work environment. This marked the beginning of our journey in the web development field, encompassing various aspects such as website creation, marketing, graphics design, and web design.

I was assigned to the web development department, which initially made me doubt my abilities. It was my first time diving into web development, and the challenge ahead seemed daunting. However, I embraced the opportunity to learn and grow. In the first few weeks of my internship, I faced a significant hurdle – a project requiring front-end development with a strong emphasis on responsive design. It was an uphill battle, and I struggled to grasp all the concepts and techniques involved. Despite the challenges, I persevered.

The process of making a website responsive proved to be both crucial and demanding. It took me two weeks of continuous learning and practice to fully comprehend the intricacies of responsive design. As an intern, it was a tremendous sense of accomplishment to finally understand how to create a responsive website. Although I had to extend the deadline for my first project to ensure its completion, the experience was incredibly fulfilling.

Working alongside the team at R Web Solutions has been an invaluable experience. The support and guidance provided by my colleagues laid a solid foundation for my future pursuits in web development. They not only taught me technical skills but also instilled in me the importance of perseverance and determination. The struggles and challenges I faced during this internship have undoubtedly made me a better person, both personally and professionally.

I am grateful for the opportunities and experiences I've had during this internship. It has ignited my passion for web development and strengthened my resolve to pursue a career in this field. As I continue on this journey, I look forward to further growth and the ability to contribute meaningfully to the web development industry.
